# Condition Grading Standards

**Status:** Active  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Grading Scale

Kollect-It uses a standardized condition grading system:

1. **Fine** - Excellent condition, minimal wear
2. **Very Good** - Good condition with minor wear
3. **Good** - Acceptable condition with visible wear
4. **Fair** - Significant wear but functional

## Rules

- Condition must be accurately described
- Photos must reflect stated condition
- Condition affects pricing and buyer expectations
- Condition cannot be changed after sale

## Implementation

- Stored in `Product.condition` field
- Displayed on product detail pages
- Used in search/filter functionality
