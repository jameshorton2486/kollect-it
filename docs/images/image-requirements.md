# Image Requirements

**Status:** Active  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

Product images must meet quality and format standards for professional presentation.

## Requirements

### Format
- **Accepted**: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- **Preferred**: JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics
- **Auto-conversion**: ImageKit converts to WebP for delivery

### Dimensions
- **Minimum**: 800x600px
- **Recommended**: 2000x1500px or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: Flexible, but 4:3 or 16:9 preferred

### Quality
- **Resolution**: High resolution for zoom functionality
- **Color**: Accurate color representation
- **Focus**: Sharp, in-focus images
- **Lighting**: Even, professional lighting

### Quantity
- **Minimum**: 1 image per product
- **Maximum**: 8 images per product
- **Primary**: First image is the main product photo
